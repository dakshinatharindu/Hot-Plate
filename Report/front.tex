\begin{abstract}
    This report is a fulfilment for the final evaluation of Laboratory Practice II (EN2090) final group project. We are designing a PID Hotplate controller with analog electronics. As most of Hot plates in the market does not have a temperature controlling mechanism, we are designing a PID feedback controlled temperature managing mechanism. It will help the user to reduce electricity consumption as well as a the life time of the hotplate. We can control the temperature upto $210 ^\circ C$ range. And the controller is designed to work in 2.5KW.
\end{abstract}
